About "CNN-IBN Indian of the Year"

The award recognizes the Indian(s) whose contribution to the country in a calendar year has strengthened the foundation of our society and has helped build Brand India in the process. 'CNN-IBN Indian of the Year' adheres to a stringent process of selection, where the Network18 Editorial Board draws the list of nominees, which is presented to the Jury who take final call on the winners across categories.
