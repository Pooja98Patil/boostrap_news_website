About "CNN-IBN Indian of the Year"

The year 2006 saw the introduction of the CNN-IBN Indian of the Year. The initiative recognizes the Indian(s) whose contribution to the country in a calendar year has strengthened the foundation of our society and has helped build Brand India in the process. Having seen nine editions since its inception, the CNN-IBN Indian of the Year has cemented its position as the biggest awards in the domain of News television and an institution of impeccable credibility.

Winners are felicitated for their contributions across categories such as:

  • Politics
  • Sports
  • Entertainment
  • Business
  • Public
  • Service
  • Global Indian

In addition to this, Special Achievement, Outstanding Achievement & Lifetime Achievement Awards are presented to other exceptional achievers.
